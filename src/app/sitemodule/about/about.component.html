About Us

The Kumolus Cloud Management Platform enables organizations to gain access to cloud services to deliver IT agility while maintaining enterprise control Founded by Michael Salleo and Josh McGrath in 2012, the Kumolus Cloud Management Platform was released in 2016. Kumolus is headquartered in Melbourne, Australia with offices in Asia and a growing network of global partners.
